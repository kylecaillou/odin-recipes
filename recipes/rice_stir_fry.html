Rice Stiry Fry

Description

This recipe is very common and can be easily made by anyone. This is made specifically for my taste, but one could easily modify the recipe if needed.

Ingredients

  • Rice
  • Broccoli
  • Mushrooms
  • Frozen peas and carrots
  • Chicken
  • Eggs

Steps

  1. Cook the rice and have it ready before cooking the rest
  2. Fry the eggs and then put aside
  3. Fry the chicken and then put aside, make sure to not overcook it or else it will become hard.
  4. Put all the vegetables together and cook until ready/soft
  5. Add in the cooked rice, chicken and eggs and mix to desired liking
  6. Make sure to season and add any flavours as see fit throughout any of the steps
  7. Serve and enjoy!
